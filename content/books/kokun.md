Uehashi provides an analysis of empire and agriculture in this fantasy land, asking questions which are similar to those posed by [James Scott in Against the Grain]({{< ref "/books/againstthegrain.md" >}}). The questions of the state and their role in agrarian systems and enforcing specific agrarian systems above others. 
<!--more-->
 The ritualization of agricultural practices leads to questions of what happens when the folk knowledge is lost, particularly in context of disease and pests. Uehashi provides a depth of commentary which focuses in on agroecology, a primer for anyone interested in the topic, all contained within a fantasy setting.
