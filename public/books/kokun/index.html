Kokun: The Girl From the West by Nahoko Uehashi

February 26, 2026

Uehashi provides an analysis of empire and agriculture in this fantasy land, asking questions which are similar to those posed by James Scott in Against the Grain. The questions of the state and their role in agrarian systems and enforcing specific agrarian systems above others.

The ritualization of agricultural practices leads to questions of what happens when the folk knowledge is lost, particularly in context of disease and pests. Uehashi provides a depth of commentary which focuses in on agroecology, a primer for anyone interested in the topic, all contained within a fantasy setting.
